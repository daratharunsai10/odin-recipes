Panipuri

Description

"Embark on a flavor-packed adventure with our Pani Puri! Crisp shells filled with tangy tamarind water, spicy potato, and fresh herbs create a symphony of taste and texture. Each bite bursts with the vibrant flavors of street food, leaving you craving more of this delightful sensation."

Ingredients

  • Semolina (sooji)
  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Potatoes
  • Tamarind pulp
  • Green chilies
  • Fresh coriander
  • Mint leaves
  • Chaat masala
  • Cumin powder
  • Black salt
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Prepare the puri dough and shape into small balls.
  2. Fry the puris until golden brown and puffed up.
  3. Mash potatoes and mix with spices.
  4. Prepare tamarind water and assemble the puris with filling.
  5. Dip filled puris in tamarind water. Serve immediately.
