Irani-chai

Description

: "Indulge in the warmth of our Irani Chai, a cherished tradition from the streets of Hyderabad. Crafted with fragrant tea leaves, creamy milk, and a dash of nostalgia, it's the perfect blend to soothe your senses and transport you to a place of comfort and relaxation."

  • Tea leaves
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil water and add tea leaves. Let it steep.
  2. Add milk and sugar. Simmer until well blended.
  3. Strain the chai into cups. Serve hot.
