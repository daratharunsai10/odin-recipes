Hyderabad-Briyani

Description

"Savor the essence of Hyderabad with our Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani! Fragrant rice, succulent chicken, and traditional spices meld together to create a culinary masterpiece. Each bite is a journey through the vibrant flavors of South India, leaving you craving more of this irresistible delight."

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chilies
  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Biryani masala
  • Saffron
  • Ghee
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices.
  3. Saute onions, tomatoes, and spices. Add chicken and cook.
  4. Layer cooked rice and chicken in a pot. Add saffron and ghee.
  5. Cover and cook until rice is tender. Serve hot.
